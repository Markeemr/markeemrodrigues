Quarantine "KEEM!"

Reflecting on my quarantine so far, the good and bad.

Quarantine has been very tough. It has challenged my mental health more times than not and probably will continue to do so. I have always been someone who struggles with being alone and the fact that I have to do it in the confines of my house makes it all the more worse. The meme of the 7 spidermans with the days of week on them all looking at each other was truly my life for about a month.

I have been trying to be productive though. Started get back into reading, making more quality music than I have ever created, and I completed 5 summer classes (NEVER AGAIN). Quarantine has been super challenging but for me it will be coming to an end now that I have to be back on campus in about 2 and a half weeks. RA training will be interesting but the fact that I will be in an environment will definitely help me overall. All I can do is just count the days.
